Case Highlight

Wing-type coating: quality you can measure

Our signature butterfly-wing coating plants move formers horizontally — eliminating the drop marks that plague vertical lines — and use 70–80 m leaching tanks that dramatically improve DMF removal. The result: higher quality gloves and falling demand for old vertical designs.

  • Palm, knuckle or full coating from the same line
  • No drop marks on the glove back
  • Modular capacity — expand former bars as you grow
