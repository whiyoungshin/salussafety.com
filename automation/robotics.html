Engineering — Robotics

Six-axis robotics for entire production lines

Not a robot in a cage — a fully robotised production line, engineered around your process from dipping to doffing.

What We Deliver

Robotisation as a turnkey service

We supply six-axis robotic plants for all kinds of production lines. Our engineers analyse the time process of each production section, then design robot placement, tooling and PLC control as one integrated system — and stay through commissioning and operator training.

  • Process time study of every production section
  • Robot cell design, tooling and end-effectors
  • PLC & safety system integration
  • On-site commissioning and training
  • Spare parts and long-term support
Project Checklist

What we need to design your plant

Process Data

Time process per production section, estimated capacity, and product samples for gripper design.

Facility Data

Factory dimensions (L × W × H), heating source, and electrical supply (380 / 415 / 440 V).

Your Team
