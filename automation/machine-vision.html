Engineering — Machine Vision

Eyes that never blink on your line

Camera-based inspection integrated directly into coating, packing and assembly machinery — catching defects at line speed.

Capability

Inspection built into the machine, not bolted on

Because we build the production machinery ourselves, vision systems are designed into the line: lighting, camera position, reject handling and PLC signalling all engineered as one. Typical deployments inspect coating coverage, drop marks, print quality and packaging completeness.

  • Coating coverage & drop-mark detection
  • Print and branding verification
  • Mask weld & ear-loop integrity checks
  • Packaging completeness before shrink-wrap
  • Reject handling integrated with PLC
