Engineering — AGV Systems

Material flow without manual muscle

Automated guided vehicles and intralogistics that link production stages into one continuous flow — designed around your factory, not a catalogue.

Capability

From knitting hall to warehouse, hands-free

In a glove factory alone, greige goods move from knitting to coating, to printing, to packing, to storage — thousands of trolley trips a month. We design AGV routes, load stations and PLC handshakes so material arrives where machines need it, when they need it.

  • Route & traffic design for your floor plan
  • Load/unload station engineering
  • PLC & machine handshake integration
  • Safety scanning and pedestrian zones
  • Phased rollout alongside existing trolleys
Where AGVs Fit

Typical deployments

Inter-Process Transfer

Knitting → coating → packing transfer without forklift traffic in production halls.

Warehouse Shuttling

Finished-goods movement to storage and despatch, integrated with wrapping lines.

Chemical Handling

Controlled movement of PU chemical drums and pigments with reduced operator exposure.
