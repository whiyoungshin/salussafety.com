Engineering — Consulting

Decide with engineering, not enthusiasm

Before you invest in a plant, our engineers turn your ambition into numbers: capacity, layout, utilities, staffing and payback.

Our Method

The same checklist we use for every plant we build

Every Salus machinery proposal starts with a disciplined data-gathering process refined over hundreds of inquiries since 2001. As a consulting service, we apply it to your whole factory.

  • Production capacity estimation & validation
  • Factory dimensioning (L × W × H) and layout
  • Heating source selection — gas, thermic oil, steam, electric
  • Electrical planning: 380 / 415 / 440 V, 50/60 Hz
  • Process choice: palm / knuckle / full, single vs double dip
  • Sample analysis and product benchmarking
