Selection Guide

Match the level to the hazard

EN388 rates cut resistance A–F by the ISO 13997 TDM test; ANSI/ISEA 105 rates A1–A9. Our Level D / A4 series is proven in stamping, glass handling and blade-adjacent work, while Level A / A2 covers general assembly with incidental sharp edges.

  • Level D / A4 — metal stamping, glass, steel slitting
  • Level A / A2 — assembly, logistics, maintenance
  • Touchscreen versions across both levels
