Why Salus Gloves

Coating technology is our unfair advantage

We build the coating plants other glove factories buy. Our own production runs on wing-type lines with horizontal former movement — no drop marks, cleaner coating edges, and 70–80 m leaching tanks for industry-leading DMF removal.

  • Silicone-free PU chemistry — safe for paint shops and electronics
  • Korean 400D HPPE yarns for consistent cut protection
  • 10 & 13 gauge SHIMA SEIKI knitting for precise fit
  • Touchscreen-conductive black PU available across ranges
  • 1.2 million pairs per month of audited capacity
